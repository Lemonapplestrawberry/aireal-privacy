Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Welcome to Aireal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Aireal collects the following information to provide air quality and pollen data:

  • Location Data: We request access to your device's location (GPS) to identify the nearest air quality monitoring station and provide localised pollen forecasts. Location data is used in real-time and is not stored on our servers.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To determine the nearest air quality monitoring station (Irceline)
  • To fetch real-time pollen data relevant to your location (Google Pollen API)
  • To display air quality index (AQI), PM2.5, PM10, and pollen levels

3. Third-Party Services

Aireal uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Irceline (Belgian Interregional Environment Agency) – provides real-time air quality data. Your approximate location coordinates are sent to our proxy server to fetch relevant station data.
  • Google Pollen API – provides pollen forecast data based on your location. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob – displays advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation. You may opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Aireal does not store your location data or personal information on any server. Location coordinates are used transiently to fetch relevant environmental data and are not logged or retained.

5. Advertising

Aireal displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalised based on your interests. To opt out of personalised advertising, visit your device's ad settings or Google's Ad Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Aireal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke location permission at any time via your device settings. Without location access, the app will not be able to provide localised air quality data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@aireal.app
